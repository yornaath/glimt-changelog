# Glimt 1.0.0 (16/04/2026)

First major release. This one is about first impressions — a fully guided onboarding experience now welcomes every new user, walking them through choosing a breathing pattern and setting up reminders before they ever reach the main app.

## New Features

- **Improved glassy look and visual improvements** across the app.
- **Onboarding flow** — New users are now guided through a step-by-step setup experience with smooth animated transitions. Choose your breathing pattern, configure your reminder interval, and activate your license — all before entering the app.

## Improvements

- Activation screen redesigned with new logo, soft gradient backgrounds, and updated button style.
- Font rendering made consistent across the micro breath reminder and notification windows.
